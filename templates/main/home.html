Welcome to Simorg Todo App

Simorg Todo is designed to help you stay organized and efficient. Our app provides a simple and intuitive way for you to manage your tasks, whether they're work-related, personal, or anything in between. With Simorg Todo, you can easily add, edit, and track tasks, ensuring you never miss a deadline.

Our goal is to provide users with a streamlined, distraction-free task management experience. The interface is designed to be minimalist, so you can focus on what matters the most: getting things done. With features like task completion tracking, prioritization, and a user-friendly design, Simorg Todo makes managing your to-do list a breeze.

We are continuously improving and expanding the app’s features based on user feedback. Our mission is to help you achieve more with less effort, so you can spend more time doing what you love, while staying productive.
